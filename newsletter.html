June 2026 – By Amme Cook

 Upcoming Eco Beauty Workshop in Sarasota 
You can care for both your hair/skin and the environment simultaneously. This is made possible by choosing plant-based skincare and cruelty-free cosmetic products. Many modern beauty products contain harmful chemicals that can irritate sensitive skin or cause non-disclosed side effects. Many cheaper shampoos and conditioners contain sulfates and silicon, these ingrediants are marketed to smooth and deep clean your hair. However, these ingrediants can dry your hair out by stripping the natural oils, lead to breakage, or even hair loss. Earth Bound Beauty believes that beauty can be easy, natural, and thoughtful. We focus on using plant-based and cruelty-free products, which are typically made from botanicals, essential oils, and natural pigments. This helps to reduce the impacts on the environment from synthetic chemicals as well as giving skin and hair the nourishment that it needs.

Seasonal Beauty Tips

  • Use mineral-based sunscreen to protect your skin without harming marine life.
  • Choose reusable makeup remover pads to reduce waste.
  • Hydrate your skin with aloe and plant oils during hot summer months.
  • Support local salons that prioritize eco-friendly beauty products.

At Earth Bound Beauty, we believe beauty routines should enhance your confidence without harming your health or the planet. Our salon strivees to expand our range of natural treatments and sustainable beauty services designed to nourish your skin and reduce environmental impact.

We invite our clients and community members to stay connected with us through workshops, beauty tutorials, and seasonal skincare tips. By learning more about sustainable beauty, you can make informed decisions that benefit both your personal wellness and the environment.
